C64 SID — Axel F

A playful take on the Commodore 64's three-channel SID chip. Press play to hear an in-browser rendition of Harold Faltermeyer's "Axel F" built from pulse, triangle, and "chaos" noise voices.

Voice Mixer

  • Pulse Lead — carries the main Axel F melody with quick filter sweeps.
  • Triangle Pad — underpins the harmony with a glassy SID triangle.
  • Chaos Drum — emulates the SID's famous "chaos" mode for crunchy drums.

Pattern Overview

The sequencer loops a 32-step grid at 124 BPM. Notes glow as they are scheduled so you can follow each channel's groove.

Built with the Web Audio API. Best experienced on desktop speakers or headphones for the wide stereo image and chunky SID grit.
